Your Perfect Oasis Awaits

Welcome to Villa Jure, a charming 137m² stone house surrounded by a 300m² garden filled with century-old olive trees. Lounge by the pool, fire up the barbecue, or enjoy dining in the cool shade. Inside, rustic beauty meets modern comfort.

A Gateway to Comfort

Villa Jure features two kitchens and four spacious en-suite rooms, offering comfort for up to 8 guests. Located at the foot of Kozjak Mountain, it’s only minutes away from the beach, Split, and the airport, making it a convenient yet tranquil retreat.

Where Fun Meets Serenity

Discover fun and relaxation with a private playground, indoor games, a gym, and high-speed Wi-Fi. Whether you’re looking for adventure or quiet moments with loved ones, Villa Jure promises an unforgettable escape for families and friends.
